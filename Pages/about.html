About Us

Know About HGPI

Kala-Amb, Distt. Sirmour (H.P), has been operating under the aegis of Maa Saraswati Educational Trust since 2002. Maa Saraswati Educational Trust is registered in the state of Haryana on 15th March 2002. The promoters of the Trust are highly qualified persons, having vast experience in the field of education, who are dedicated to produce excellent and quality technical education based on moral values and ethics. The main objective of the trust is to develop skilled and educated manpower in the area where there is acute shortage.

In a very small span, the Trust has been able to achieve success and command respect in the eyes of the people of the region with its non-profit educational and charitable activities in the region. The aim of the trust is to develop efficient manpower especially in the field of Law, Education, Nursing, Pharmacy, Management, Computer Science Engineering, Skill Development and Health.
